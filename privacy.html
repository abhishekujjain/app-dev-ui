Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Intelligent Mind handles information for Android apps, including Latitude Longitude Finder and Local Places Map.

Information We Access

  • Location data: Used to show your current position and nearby places/features.
  • Device/network data: May be used by platform services (for example, Google Maps SDK) to function correctly.

How Data Is Used

  • To provide map rendering, coordinate tools, and nearby places discovery.
  • To improve reliability, performance, and app experience.

Data Sharing

The apps may rely on trusted third-party services such as Google Play Services and Google Maps. Please review Google policies for details about their data practices.

Your Choices

  • You can deny location permission from Android settings at any time.
  • You can uninstall the apps whenever you want.

Contact

For privacy questions, please contact us through your Google Play developer contact channel.
